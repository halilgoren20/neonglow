⚡ Privacy Policy

Neon Glow Blaze  |  Last updated: March 15, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Neon Glow Blaze ("we", "our", or "the app") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our mobile game.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Account Information: If you sign in with Google, we receive your name, email address, and profile picture from Google.
  • Game Progress Data: Level completions, scores, stars, achievements, and streaks.
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version, and language settings.
  • Usage Data: How you interact with the app, session duration, and features used.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To save and sync your game progress across devices
  • To display your in-game statistics and achievements
  • To improve the game experience
  • To serve advertisements (see section below)

3. Advertising

Neon Glow Blaze is a free app supported by advertising. We may use third-party advertising services (such as Google AdMob) to display ads. These services may collect and use data about your device and usage to serve relevant advertisements.

You can learn more about how Google uses your data at: policies.google.com/privacy

4. Google Sign-In

If you choose to sign in with Google, authentication is handled securely by Google. We only receive basic profile information (name, email, profile photo) to create your in-game account. We do not have access to your Google password.

5. Data Storage

Your game data may be stored on secure cloud servers (such as Firebase by Google) to enable progress syncing. Data is stored securely and protected using industry-standard encryption.

6. Third-Party Services

The app may use the following third-party services:

  • Google Sign-In (Authentication)
  • Firebase (Cloud storage)
  • Google AdMob (Advertising)

7. Children's Privacy

Neon Glow Blaze is rated 4+ and is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without parental consent. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us.

8. Data Retention

We retain your game data for as long as your account is active. You can request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

9. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction or deletion of your data
  • Opt out of personalized advertising
  • Play the game without creating an account (Guest mode)

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of significant changes by updating the date at the top of this page.
